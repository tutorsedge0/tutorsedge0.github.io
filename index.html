Mission Statement

The tutors here at Tutors Edge know how hard it is to find a quality tutoring service at an afforable rate.
We also know that during this pandemic a lot of student's academic careers have been negatively impacted.
That is why we have decided to create this non-profit organization that gives free tutoring service for students in the grades 3 to 10.
